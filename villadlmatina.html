WELCOME to Villa Dalmatina

oasis of piece and serenity

Located at the center of the Pelješac peninsula.

This beautiful holiday home is located on the edge of the small village of Sreser, on the Pelješac peninsula, right on a pebble beach. The interior and exterior of the holiday home are tastefully decorated with stylish details. You can spend unforgettable hours outdoors on the terrace. In the garden, there is a barbecue where you can prepare delicious specialties with your loved ones while your children play freely in the nearby playground.
