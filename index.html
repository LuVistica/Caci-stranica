WELCOME to Villa Dalmatina

oasis of piece and comfort

Located at the center of the Pelješac


This beautiful holiday home is located on the edge of the small village of Sreser, on the Pelješac peninsula, right on a pebble beach. The interior and exterior of the holiday home are tastefully decorated with stylish details. You can spend unforgettable hours relaxing indoors or outdoors on the terrace.

About Villa


The property boasts a range of amenities to enhance your stay. Enjoy the outdoor pool, jacuzzi, and barbecue area where you can prepare delicious meals while your children play safely in the adjacent playground. There's also a large artificial grass playground perfect for volleyball and mini football, a ping pong table, and a foosball table. Indoors you have a private gym with a multifuctional machine, running bike and treadmill. And also for a absoulte relaxation inside of a house you have sauna at your disposal.

BEST Holiday Home Beach House

Villa Dalmatina was honored with the Best Holiday Beach House award in 2017 by the European Holiday Home Association, highlighting its excellence as a top

vacation destination.

Private Beach and Water Activities


In front of the house is a private beach just 6 meters away, equipped with sun loungers, umbrellas, and a pier. For those who love water activities, five kayaks are available as part of the villa's amenities, allowing you to explore the beautiful coastline at your leisure.

Local Attractions and Activities


As a mode of transportation, quad and a buggy are at your disposal for exploring the surrounding area. Additionally, the villa offers seven bicycles for use. Explore the unique landscape and taste local wines Dingač and Postupa. Food lovers can indulge in delicious mussels and oysters, freshly sourced from the area.

Pets are WELCOME

We warmly welcome pets in our villa, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable stay

for all members of your family, including your furry friends.

Space and Comfort


The villa itself covers around 320 square meters with an additional 1000 square meters of garden space, offering plenty of room for relaxation and outdoor activities.The villa's owner lives in a neighboring house and is available to provide guests with more information and assistance during their stay.
